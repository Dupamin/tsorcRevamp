'''The Story of Red Cloud''' is a massive Dark Souls-inspired adventure built inside the world of Terraria using a custom map, mod and companion soundtrack. Your journey will span 17 dungeons spread across an expansive, hand-crafted open world, complete with over 600 items and 200 NPCs (including 22 new bosses!) to offer a 20 to 30+ hr experience for a first playthrough.

This wiki details the modern edition of the mod for Terraria 1.4. For the tConfig version, see the [https://the-story-of-red-cloud.fandom.com/wiki/Welcome! Fandom wiki] instead.
